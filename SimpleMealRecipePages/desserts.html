Boozy Baked Bananas

Ingredients
6 ripe bananas, peeled and sliced in half
2 tbsp dark brown sugar
2 tbsp honey
40g walnuts, toasted and chopped
vanilla ice cream, to serve

Method

Heat the oven to 220C/200C fan.

Toss the banana halves in an ovenproof dish with the sugar and honey.

Bake for 15 mins, then remove from the oven.

Heat the grill to high and grill the bananas for 3-5 mins.

Spoon the bananas into bowls and scatter over the walnuts.

Serve with ice cream, and honey from the dish drizzled over the top.
