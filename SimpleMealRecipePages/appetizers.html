Epic Summer Salad

Ingredients
400g black beans , drained
2 large handfuls baby spinach leaves , roughly chopped
500g heritage tomatoes , chopped into large chunks
1 large red onion , halved and finely sliced
2 avocados , peeled and sliced
100g feta , crumbled

Method

Heat the oil in a pan, then soften the onion for 5 mins without browning.Make the dressing by blending all of the ingredients in a food processor

Scatter the beans and spinach over a large platter.

Arrange the tomatoes, mango and onion on top and gently toss together with your hands.

Top the salad with the avocados, feta and herbs.
